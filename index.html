Welcome to Big Wave Amusement Park



Come play in living color...open year-round, rain or shine, day and night...at the Boston's only Water Park!

The Bigwave Water Park is available to the public for daily admission. The 30,000 square foot water park includes 4 water slides, a 300 foot "stormy" river with waterfalls, rapids and a spouting humpback whale; Pirate Bay kiddie area with dumping buckets, water cannons and smaller slides, an indoor wave pool, a 24 person Jacuzzi, the Gazebo Cafe for snacks along with changing rooms and lockers available to rent. Open daily all year round.
